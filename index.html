About Me

I’m Yoshiki Takeuchi, an electrical engineering graduate from Purdue University, specializing in semiconductor research and cleanroom fabrication. I’ll be pursuing a master’s degree at Texas A&M, focusing on electrical engineering with a concentration in semiconductors.

My recent internships at TSMC and Micron involved process integration and advanced packaging, where I honed my skills in cutting-edge semiconductor technologies.

Outside of work, I’m passionate about exploring innovative ideas, staying active at the gym, playing the violin, and participating in community activities that foster creativity and growth. Let’s connect and drive innovation together!
